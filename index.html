About

Remembering to take medication can be a struggle for anyone. That is why Pacoon is here to jump right in and take care of your routine! With its intuitive interface, easy-to-red reminders, and built-in features for accessibility, you'll never forget to take your medication again.

And don't worry, Pacoon was designed to be accessible for anyone and open free source so there is no data tracking, its completely secure and confidential.

Accessibility

Ease on reading should be comforted for everyone. Pacoon ensures the typography used is a minimum of 18px and maintains a contrast between text and backgrounds, including those with visual impairments. Which is why there is a Toggle button.

Equal care was taken on the gentle animation and hover options that do not create noise or confuse the eye focus.
